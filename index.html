Seoul Soondae Restaurant, located in Markham St in Annandale, is dedicated to bringing the comforting flavors of Korea to our community. For over 30 years, our esteemed chef has passionately crafted our specialty dish, Soondae, along with many other soup dishes and Korean’s favorite home dishes. At Seoul Soondae, every meal is an invitation to create lasting memories with friends and family, all while enjoying the delightful tastes of Korean cuisine along with your favorite drinks!
